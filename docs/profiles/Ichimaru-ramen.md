## Ichimaru-ramen

`Ichimaru Ramen` is a Japanese regional chain of ramen, a Japanese noodle dish, who marks its 20th anniversary. It currently operates 80 stores across the region, beloved locally for its house-made noodles and broth and supported by many regulars. 


### Business strategical goal in three years

The president has set a strategic goal: “expand to 100 stores within three years and grow operating profit by 30%.” His message is clear — no race to the bottom on price; the company will keep being chosen for its distinctive taste and brand.


### Food delivery model

Ichimaru Ramen uses a central-kitchen model: 2 factories centrally produce noodles, broth, and toppings and ship them to each store. Four area managers draft a weekly delivery plan the prior week, then confirm, adjust, and finalize the next day’s volumes the day before.
